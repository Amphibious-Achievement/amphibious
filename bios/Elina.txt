Elina hails from Oshkosh, Wisconsin, where she grew up and was actively involved with her community. Between being Sports Editor for her high school's newspaper and concertmaster of the Oshkosh Youth Symphony Orchestra, Elina still found time for her passion for teaching by founding a violin program for elementary school kids at the local Boys and Girls club. Elina has been swimming competitively since she was eight years old and is very familiar with keeping the balance between athletics and academics. She is currently captain of MIT's varsity swimming and diving team and is majoring in mechanical engineering and pursuing a minor in psychology. Outside of school and the pool, Elina likes to eat cheese, lift weights, paint, and play with rubber duckies.
