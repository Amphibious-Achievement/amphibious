Teri was born and raised in East Lansing, MI. Teri played water polo and swam in high school for four years and was on the ski team for two years. Teri started to swim when she was just a baby and has loved the water ever since. In high school she spent time tutoring younger kids in her school district twice a week and grew to love helping other people learn. Teri is currently a freshman at Massachusetts Institute of Technology and Captain of the Women's Club Water Polo Team. In her free time, Teri enjoys organizing surprise parties, watching movies, and wandering around Boston with friends.
