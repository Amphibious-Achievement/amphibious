Duane was born in Baltimore, Maryland where he grew up and attended Baltimore City Public School system before coming to the Massachusetts Institute of Technology.  While in high school Duane was a member of an organization that taught high school students Algebra in preparation for state exams.  Duane also swam for four years in high school and was the captain of his team in his senior year.  Duane is a certified lifeguard and swim instructor.  He has taught kids ranging from ages 3-15 and levels from novice to expert to swim at the Rose Bowl Aquatics Center located in Pasadena, California.
