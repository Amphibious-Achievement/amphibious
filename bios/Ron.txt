Ron was born and raised in Newton, Massachusetts. Throughout his life, Ron always loved being in the water. It was no surprise, then, that Ron joined a competitive club swim team at only seven years old and remained a member there for the next ten years. By the end of high school, Ron’s passion for swimming and dedication to the sport allowed him to achieve national status as a US Open and Senior National qualifier in the backstroke events. Since 2008, Ron has coached for the local YMCA and for private clients, helping athletes of all levels achieve their goals in and out of the water.
 
Ron’s favorite hobbies are generally aquatic &#45 swimming, surfing, waterskiing &#45 but he also loves skiing, hiking, and black and white photography.
