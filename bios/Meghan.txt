Meghan Torrence grew up in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. She is a current member of the Varsity Women's Lightweight Crew Team at MIT after rowing three years for a high school club team, Three Rivers Rowing Association. She was a member of the first boat from TRRA to ever attend US Rowing's Youth National Championship in the Varsity 8+ category. Meghan also volunteered with the Three Rivers Adaptive Rowing Program as a coach. She served as captain of her high school's varsity soccer team and concert master of her high school's orchestra. Meghan is planning to major in Computational Biology at MIT. She loves to listen to music, bake, and read in her spare time.
