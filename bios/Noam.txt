Noam was born in Brookline, Massachusetts where he grew up until he moved to Israel and then back to Boston upon attending the Massachusetts Institute of Technology.  Noam rowed for four years in high school and was the captain of his team in his senior year.  Noam has coached for Brookline High School at the Riverside Boat Club along the Charles since February of 2010.  He led his two top novice eight rowing shells to state championship gold at the MPSRAs in the spring of 2010. In the spring of 2011, Noam led his top two novice eights to state championships bronze medals, scoring championship medals across all boats for the second year in a row.

Noam's hobbies include coaching crew and rowing himself, snowboarding, reading economics books, giving tours of MIT, researching healthcare and eating.
