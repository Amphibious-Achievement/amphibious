Lila was born and raised in Seattle, WA where she lived before moving to Boston to attend MIT. Lila grew up swimming and sailing in summer leagues and for her high school team, but had a passion for rowing. She rowed for 4 years in high school, and after competing at the USRowing Junior National Championship Regatta in 2008, she was team captain for her Senior year. Lila also competed in the Women's Varsity 8+ boat at MIT for two years. She embraces the lifestyle of the great Pacific Northwest, and enjoys hiking, running, sailing, and baking on rainy days.
