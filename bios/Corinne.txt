Corinne was born in Boston but spent most of her life in a small town in Maine.  She played tennis and swam in high schools, and was the captain of her swim team in her senior year. She was a camp counselor for a summer job where she taught swimming to children from 6-12 years old.  Corinne also loves to tutor, having done so formally and informally for many years throughout high school and college.  She is currently a tutor at MIT for chemistry.

Corinne enjoys reading for pleasure, exploring foreign cities, hiking, skydiving, and napping in the sun.
